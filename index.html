PHOENIX TRANSIT ORBITS

About Phoenix Transit Orbits

A real-time visualization of Phoenix Valley Metro light rail trains, mapped onto a solar system metaphor. Each orbit ring represents a light rail route, named after a planet. The glowing dots are actual train vehicles moving along their routes.

Visual cues: Dots glow brighter and leave longer trails at higher speeds. Dots jitter while in transit and sit still when approaching or stopped at a station. The central sun pulses with a 3-second breathing cycle. Stars are static decoration.

Audio: Optional ambient music from Soma.fm Deep Space One — toggle at bottom-left.

Data: Live vehicle positions from Valley Metro's GTFS-realtime feed, polled every 7 seconds via a Cloudflare Worker proxy.
